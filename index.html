KOBE BRYANT

The Mamba

Here's the timeline of Kobe Bryant's Life

  • 1992-1996 - Played for LOWER MERION HIIGH SCHOOL.
  • 1996 - Drafted 13th in NBA Draft for Charlotte Hornets then sent to The Lakers.
  • 1999 - Won the first NBA Champtionship.
  • 2000 & 2001 - Won another 2 Champtionship and became the team who won 3-peat after Micheal Jordan's Bulls.
  • 2007 - Won Gold for America in the Olympics.
  • 2008 - NBA Most Valuable Player.
  • 2009 & 2010 - Won 2 Champtionship as well as NBA Finals MVP.
  • 2012 - Won another Gold for Americain the Olympics.
  • 2016 - Retired from Professional Basketball Career with 33,643 Points, 7,047 Rebounds and 6,306 Assist in the NBA.
  • 2020 - Died in the Helicopter Crash with his 13-year old daughter Giana Bryant and 6 others on 26th January.
